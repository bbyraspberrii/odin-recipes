Orange Creamsicle Overnight Oats

What are orange creamsicle overnight oats?

Orange creamsicle is a blend of vanilla ice cream with orange sherbet. It can either be swirly, mixed together or as orange sherbet with an ice cream center.

Overnight oats are raw oats that have been soaked in milk(most often) for at least 2 hours or, overnight. They become thick and creamy and is a super healthy option for breakfast/dessert!

This orange creamsicle version of overnight oats is creamy, citrusy, and extremely easy. It's a take on my favorite childhood orange creamsicle bars - but in the form of overnight oats!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1/2 whole milk*
  • 1/4 cup greek yogurt
  • 1/2 juiced blood orange*
  • 1/2 sliced blood orange*
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • More blood orange slices (for topping; optional)

*You can use whichever oranges or milk.

Steps

  1. Add rolled oats, milk, greek yogurt, juiced and sliced blood oranges, chia seeds, and vanilla extract into a jar or bowl. Stir and mix well.
  2. Refrigerate overnight or for 8 hours.
  3. Top with fresh orange slices and drizzle honey.
