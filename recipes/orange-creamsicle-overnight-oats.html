Orange Creamsicle Overnight Oats

What are orange creamsicle overnight oats?

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1/2 whole milk
  • 1/4 cup greek yogurt
  • 1 juiced mandarin orange
  • 1 sliced mandarin orange
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • More mandarin orange slices (optional)

Steps

  1. Add rolled oats, milk, greek yogurt, juiced and sliced mandarin oranges, chia seeds, and vanilla extract into a jar or bowl. Stir and mix well.
  2. Refrigerate overnight or for 8 hours.

  3. Top with fresh orange slices and drizzle honey.
